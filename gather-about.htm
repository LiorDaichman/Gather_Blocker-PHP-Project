Gather Blocker is a local search engine to help businesses and customers alike.
Gather Blocker allows you to find points of interest, businesses, events and a huge variety of search areas.
The purpose of the site is to help businesses during the corona period to comply with state laws in accordance with the purple mark and the health of citizens.
Our desire is to develop a tool that helps people and businesses alike such as managing their business content properly and presenting appropriate information about the amount of people who are in real time instead of customers and thus maximizing profits as much as possible during this period.
We want to give customers a sense of security and provide customers with a secure shopping experience and real-time information about which businesses to reach.
